001Identity

VOID is a Paris-based directors and post-production studio founded in 2024. We operate at the intersection of craft and technology — building images that live between the real and the rendered. 08 people, one obsession: precision.

002Philosophy

Every frame is a decision. We reject the arbitrary and commit to the intentional. Whether we're grading a fashion campaign or building a 3D world from scratch, the same logic applies: control everything you can, then let the rest breathe.
